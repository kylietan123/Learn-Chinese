What is HSK...?

HSK, which stands for Hanyu Shuiping Kaoshi, is a standard Chinese language proficiency test which consists of 6 levels, from HSK level 1 to level 6. HSK 1 is the most basic HSK where the material is about introducing Mandarin, pronunciation, tones and writing basic Hanzi letters. It consists of 150 vocabularies. The HSK 1 test consists of listening and reading questions with a total of 40 questions and must be done in 35 minutes. The problem consists of hanzi and pinyin.
