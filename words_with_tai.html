Expressing too or very with 太 (tài)

In Mandarin, the simplest way to express things that are excessive is to use the word 太 (tài). Don't forget to pay attention to add the word 了 (le) after an adjective, this is purposeful to keep Mandarin pronunciation feeling more natural.

Structure

太 + Adj. + 了

Example

  • 米饭太多了
    mǐfàn tài duō le
    Too much rice

  • 现在太晚了
    xiànzài tài wǎn le
    It's too late now

  • 老板太忙了
    lǎobǎn tài máng le
    The boss is too busy

  • 这个厕所太脏了
    zhege cèsuǒ tài zāng le
    This toilet is too dirty
