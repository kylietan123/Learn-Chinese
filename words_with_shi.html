Connecting nouns with 是 (shì)

是 | shì is a verb and is used to connect two nouns. In English it is often translated as "is".

Structure

Noun 1 + 是 + Noun 2

Example

  • 我是学生。
    wǒ shì xuéshēng
    I am a student

  • 他们是有钱人。
    tāmen shì yǒu qián rén
    They are rich people.

  • 您是老师吗？
    nín shì lǎoshī ma
    Are you a teacher ?

  • 这都是你的钱。
    zhe dōu shì nǐ de qián
    This is all your money.

  • 我也是他的朋友。
    wǒ yě shì nǐ de péngyou
    I am also his friend.
