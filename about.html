About us

We are a group of international students studying at ECUST(East China University of Science and Technology) dedicated to helping others like us study chinese. We are lead by our instructor, Teacher Yu(于涛), who is a chinese professer here at ECUST teaching both undergraduate students and short term Chinese programs. She has a lot of experience in teaching beginners who are zero-based. This group currently consists of 3 students: one from Malaysia, one from Myanmar and one from Poland. Currently, 2 of us are juniors and one is a sophomore. One study's media (she is the one who made all the videos) and the other two study's Computer Science (did the coding part).
Hopefully this website has assisted whomever has accessed it! Good luck on your studies!
