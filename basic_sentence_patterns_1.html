Learn Basic Sentence Structure
And Grammar In Mandarin

Why is it necessary to learn basic sentence structure and grammar in Mandarin?

Sentence structure in Mandarin is the easiest chapter to start the process of adopting this foreign language.
The reasons are:

  • It is a basic part of Mandarin
  • It is relatively simple and can help build self-confidence
  • Learning basic sentences can help you understand the quality of grammar

The most confusing part of grammar in Mandarin is the formation of sentence structure. But fortunately, Mandarin grammar does not have tenses or conjugations.
