Combining nouns and adjectives using 很(hěn)

We often see nouns that have adjectives, like the sentence "new book" (Book is a noun and new is an adjective) and many more. In Mandarin we can use nouns as connectors against an adjective or noun with a verb.This time we will know how to connect nouns with adjectives using 很(hěn).

Structure

Noun + 很 + Adjective

Example

  • 我很好
    wǒ hěn hǎo
    I am very good

  • 你很漂亮
    nǐ hěn piàoliǎng
    You are very beautiful

  • 他很高兴
    tā hěn gāoxìng
    He is very happy

  • 你家也很远吗 ?
    nǐ jiā yě hěn yuǎn ma ?
    Is your house also really far ?

  • 我哥哥也很高
    wǒ gēgē yě hěn gāo
    My brother is very tall
